Doo-Doong Samulnori

Doo-Doong Samulnori is a Korean percussion ensemble based in Dublin, dedicated to sharing the energy and spirit of samulnori with local and international audiences. Samulnori (literally "playing four things") is a vibrant genre of traditional Korean music performed with four core instruments:

The Four Instruments

  • Kkwaenggwari – a small gong with a piercing, commanding sound;
  • Jing – a large gong that produces deep, resonant tones;
  • Janggu – an hourglass-shaped drum offering rhythmic versatility;
  • Buk – a barrel drum that drives the ensemble's powerful beat.

Rooted in the rhythms of Korean rural folk traditions, samulnori has evolved into a celebrated contemporary art form performed on stages worldwide. With its driving beats, dynamic textures, and moments of both intensity and playfulness, it captures the essence of community, celebration, and the natural cycles of life. Through our performances, Doo-Doong Samulnori brings this rich cultural heritage to Ireland, offering audiences an unforgettable glimpse into one of Korea's most dynamic and iconic musical traditions.
